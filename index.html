About Us

Action Ink. is an innovative IT consultancy supporting NGOs with limited resources in creating and managing critical IT projects. By linking qualified university student volunteers to well defined projects at international organisations, Action Ink. supports both studentâ€™s professional development and the delivery of projects that would have otherwise been impossible to deliver.

Student Volunteers

A select group of post-graduate students volunteer their time during semester at Action Ink., supported and managed by the experienced team at Action Lab. Students gain valuable experience working in real-world IT projects for international NGOs, whilst producing concrete examples of work for their CV.

Sustainable IT Projects

Action Ink. works with organisations to design projects that map to the skills and experience of students and capacity of the team, to deliver complete and sustainable IT projects.

Our Partners

We are proud to be partnering with the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent (through Solferino Academy) to support National RC societies across the world.

Oversight Team

At Action Ink. we aim to be a transparent and accountable initiative, recognising that our primary responsibility is to both the organisations we work with, and the students that work within Action Ink.

To maintain such accountability, we have an oversight team who meet twice a year to review Action Ink.â€™s:
  • Current project portfolio
  • Working practices
  • Student wellbeing and support
  • External organisational relationships
This team comprises members from across the university and NGO sector, whose primary responsibility is to the students and organisations we work with.

This team receives a summary report from the Action Ink. leadership team prior to each meeting, and can propose recommendations or suggestions for improvement as appropriate.
